Privacy Policy for OnlyBudget

Last Updated: October 21, 2025

Thank you for choosing OnlyBudget! Protecting your privacy is fundamental to me. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use my mobile application.

Information I Collect

Personal Information

Authentication and Account Information (Required only for Google Drive Backup):

  • Google Sign-In is required only if you want to use the Google Drive backup feature.
  • The app works completely offline without any account for local usage.
  • If you choose to sign in, authentication data is used solely for secure backup access and session validation.
  • Authentication data is stored securely on your device and minimally in Cloud Firestore for session management.
  • I do not have direct access to your Google account credentials.
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings.

Data stored in Cloud Firestore (for session management only):

  • User ID (UID)
  • Google Drive refresh token

Data stored locally on your device:

  • User ID (UID)
  • Email address
  • Google Drive access token (encrypted)
  • Google Drive refresh token (encrypted)

Financial Data

All your financial data is stored locally on your device and never sent to any server.

I do not have access to your financial data unless you explicitly choose to back it up to your Google Drive.

Google Drive Backup (Optional)

  • Your financial data is backed up to your personal Google Drive account.
  • Backups are stored in the hidden AppData folder of your Google Drive.
  • I do not have access to your Google Drive or the backed-up data.
  • The backup is stored in your own Google Drive storage space.
  • Google Drive access tokens are encrypted and stored securely on your device.
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings.

How I Use Your Information

Authentication Data

  • Your email address is used solely for authentication purposes.
  • Your UID and Google Drive refresh token are stored in Cloud Firestore to maintain your Google Drive session.
  • Google Drive tokens are encrypted and stored securely using Android’s DataStore.

Financial Data

  • Your financial data is stored only on your device and is never sent to any external server.
  • If you enable Google Drive backup, your data is backed up only to your personal Google Drive.
  • I have no access to your financial information.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Financial data is stored locally using Android’s secure Room database.
  • Authentication tokens are encrypted using DataStore.
  • Your device security (screen lock, encryption) provides additional protection.

Cloud Firestore

  • Only UID and Google Drive refresh token are stored.
  • Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.
  • Firestore is protected by Google Cloud infrastructure.

Google Drive Backup

  • Data is transmitted securely over HTTPS/TLS.
  • Backup remains under your control in your Google Drive.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In API
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Google Drive API

Your Rights and Choices

  • You can view, edit, or delete local transactions at any time.
  • You can enable or disable Google Drive backup.
  • You can delete all data using “Reset All Data”.
  • You can revoke Google Drive access anytime.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
